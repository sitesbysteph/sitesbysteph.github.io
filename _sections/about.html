About

Like many of you, I have aspirations to be my own boss. My love for autonomy and desire to create is part of the reason I fell in love with tech and programming as well.

Since 2016, I've worked side jobs helping companies and nonprofits maintain their websites by updating code or designing new layouts and graphics. In 2018, I started my career as a full time software engineer, where I do full stack development to maintain and update a suite of web applications. I work mainly in Javascript and Golang these days.

Working in an enterprise setting has made me realize that I really miss the creative process of designing websites for small businesses and nonprofits. I love meeting new people and learning about their businesses. My goal is to create a website that feels.
